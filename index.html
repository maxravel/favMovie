Oldboy

All time best Korean action movie.

What I like about that movie:
  • Breathtaking action
  • So interesting plot
  • Nice music

Movie was directed in 2003 by Chan Wook Park. It's about guy who was in private jail for 15 years, he don't know why. During the movie he is trying to figure out the reason of it. It is very hard to do, in his way there are many deadly enemies. Will he survive? Will he get to know the answer? Watch it!
